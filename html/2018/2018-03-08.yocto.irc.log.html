learningcWhat's the difference between CORE_IMAGE_EXTRA_INSTALL and IMAGE_INSTALL?01:31

kergothlearningc: CORE_IMAGE_EXTRA_INSTALL is specifically intended for *user* use, i.e. in local.conf.
